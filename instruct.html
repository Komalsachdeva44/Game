Game Instructions

Objective

The objective of the game is to shoot the flying Doraemon to score points before your bullets run out.

How to Play

Click on Doraemon: Click on Doraemon to shoot him and gain points.

Clicking Outside: Clicking anywhere else on the screen will use up a bullet without gaining points.

Bullets: You start with 5 bullets. Keep an eye on your bullet count!

Scoring

Each successful hit on Doraemon increases your score by 1.

The game ends when you run out of bullets. Try to achieve the highest score possible!
